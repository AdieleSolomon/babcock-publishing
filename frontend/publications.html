Babcock University Press / Publications

Publication direction at a glance

BU Press supports academic and institutional materials in multiple formats, from formal research output to campus-facing print work.

  • Books and academic titles for teaching and scholarship.
  • Journals, magazines, and periodicals for regular communication.
  • Yearbooks, branded print, and official university materials.
Formats

What BU Press can help publish

This section translates the broader publication mentions from the legacy source into a cleaner format-based catalog.

Textbooks and monographs

Core academic books developed for classroom use, reference work, or specialist teaching within the university and beyond.

Journals and periodicals

Research-oriented publications and recurring periodicals that communicate scholarly output and institutional thought.

Student and training materials

Exercise books, learning materials, and structured print support for student-facing programs and workshops.

Corporate and administrative print

Official documents, file jackets, invitations, branded cards, and communication materials for institutional use.

Magazines, yearbooks, and newspapers

Richer print formats for university storytelling, annual records, publicity, and event documentation.

Packaging and merchandise print

Gift wraps, product packaging, and other presentation-focused print outputs handled through the press workflow.
