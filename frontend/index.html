What the press does

BU Press serves as Babcock University's formal publishing arm. It supports book development, academic dissemination, journal and periodical visibility, and broader print execution across the university environment.

The legacy source calls BU Press an integrated press system. That idea now drives the entire public structure of this rebuild.
