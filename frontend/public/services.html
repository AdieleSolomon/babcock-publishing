Services

Publishing and print services arranged as one connected system.

The legacy BU Press source presents the press as an integrated operation. This rebuilt service page keeps that core idea and turns it into a cleaner public-facing services overview.

Babcock University Press / Services

From manuscript to print delivery

BU Press supports editorial development, design preparation, production, and specialized print outputs for academic and institutional needs.

  • Editorial improvement before production begins.
  • Design, pre-press, and finishing under one service umbrella.
  • Consultancy and training for publishing and print operations.
Service Areas

Core BU Press capabilities

These service categories are drawn directly from the legacy BU Press home page and reorganized into a modern card system.

Publishing and editorial

List building, text development, copyediting, and proofreading for manuscripts that need stronger structure and readability.

Graphics and art direction

Visual development, layout direction, and pre-press design that prepares books and print jobs for production.

Finishing and bindery

Spiral binding, case binding, perfect binding, saddle stitching, rebinding, and other finishing services.

Lithography and imaging

Digital imaging, colour separation, filming, plating, and related print-preparation processes.

Print solutions

Corporate documents, exercise books, file jackets, invitations, flyers, posters, bills, journals, magazines, newspapers, and yearbooks.

Training and consultancy

Publishing and print production training, seminars, workshops, feasibility studies, and advisory support.

Paper packaging

Gift wrapping, product packaging, parcels, bond paper, and newsprint support for institutional and commercial needs.

Institutional project support

Structured support for academic departments, administrative units, conferences, and university-wide communication projects.

End-to-end coordination

Editorial, design, production, and finishing can be planned as one workflow instead of separate disconnected jobs.

Why this matters

A stronger service story than the old single-column list

The original BU Press page already had strong raw information, but the service list was easy to miss. This rebuild lifts those offerings into visible categories so visitors can quickly understand what the press actually does.

  • Academic publishing support for books, journals, and research outputs.
  • Commercial and institutional print execution for university needs.
  • Training pathways for teams that need publishing or print competence.
