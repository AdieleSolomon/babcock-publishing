Solutions

Solution pathways for everyone involved in academic knowledge work

This page translates the multi-audience structure used by large publishing sites into Babcock’s context: researchers, librarians, faculty, students, administrators, and external partners all get a clearer entry point.

How to use this page
  • Find the audience group that matches your goal.
  • Review the services, tools, and resources connected to that role.
  • Move into publishing, products, or support when you are ready.

Researcher & Author

Manuscript preparation, submission guidance, rights support, book development, and visibility planning.

Editor & Reviewer

Editorial workflows, peer review coordination, decision support, reviewer onboarding, and policy guidance.

Librarian & Academic Leader

Product visibility, usage reporting, resource discovery, and service planning for institutional teams.

Student & Faculty

Training resources, academic writing workshops, learning tools, and classroom publishing support.

Institutional Publishing Units

Operational support for journals, monographs, conference proceedings, and campus research dissemination.

Partners & Impact Makers

Technical publishing, knowledge products, and collaborative projects that convert research into practical outcomes.
